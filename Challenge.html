About Society for
Conversion GIS Membership

If you are a GIS professional or are interested in using Gis
for conservation, you can become a member of SCGIS!
The diverse membership includes members from many
different countries in the world. Join this expanding
network today!

Benefits

  • Be part of a dynamic community of passionate GIS
    conservationists that motivate and support each other
    Engage in discussions,seek advice and get GIS
    technical support from other members on our website
    and listserv
  • Access years of knowledge from past listserv email
    conservations via our listserv archive
    Get discounted registration for the SCGIS Annual
    Conference
  • Attend free webinars offered to share technical and
    conservation knowledge with SCGIS members around
    the world
  • Create a member profile, browse other member profiles,
    and send private messages to members
  • Post descriptions of the GIS projects that you are
    working on
  • Create or join an SCGIS chapter
  • Browse and post GIS-related jobs on the jobs board
    Post GIS-related news and events
